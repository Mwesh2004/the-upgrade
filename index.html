About The Newsletter

Built For
Real
People

Most content online talks to people in San Francisco or London. The Upgrade is different. It's built for Kenyans — the student in campus trying to figure out life, the young professional navigating a broken job market, the person carrying more than their share.

Every week, one honest piece on something that matters. No motivation speech. No fake guru energy. Just real talk about real things — with Kenyan banter built in, because life is ngori enough without boring newsletters.

  • Weekly issues every Monday morning
  • Topics spanning life, money, identity & mental health
  • Written with Kenyan context and banter
  • No ads. No sponsors. No performance.
  • One honest question per issue to sit with
  • Always free to subscribe
